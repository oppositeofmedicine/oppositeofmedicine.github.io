What Is OOM?

Opposite Of Medicine (short for 'The Opposite Of Have A Taste Of Your Own Medicine Is Don't Get High Off Your Own Supply') Records is a DIY label from Onaway, Michigan. The name was taken from a song on the EP 'A For Anal' by Necrotic Desires.
