What Is OOM?

N/A

Opposite Of Medicine Records (short for "The Opposite Of Have A Taste Of Your Own Medicine Is Don't Get High Off Your Own Supply Records") is a DIY label from Northern-Michigan, founded in 2025. While it's currently just a platform for me and my friends to release music, I plan to grow the label so I can actually help small bands.
